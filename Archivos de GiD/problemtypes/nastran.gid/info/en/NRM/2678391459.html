Nastran reference manual : Enforced Displacements (Points, Lines, Surfaces)

MENU SEQUENCE:
Data → Boundary Conditions → Constraints


Assigns enforced displacements or rotations. The signs convention for components of a vector follows the right hand rule (see figure below).
This condition can be assigned over points, lines and surfaces, and it is attached to nodes of the entity.

Figure 1 – Positive Signs Convention


Enforced displacements and rotations always make references to global axes.
