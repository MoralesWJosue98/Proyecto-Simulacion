
Compilation

To compile in Linux - 64 bits
----------------------------

gcc -m64 -o cmas2d_iga-linux-64.exe cmas2d_iga.c -lm

To compile in Mac OS X - general
----------------------------

gcc -o cmas2d_iga-macosx-64.exe cmas2d_iga.c -lm

To compile in Linux - 32 bits
----------------------------

gcc -m32 -o cmas2d_iga-linux-32.exe cmas2d_iga.c -lm
