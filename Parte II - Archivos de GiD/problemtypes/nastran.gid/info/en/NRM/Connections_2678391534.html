Nastran reference manual : Connections

MENU SEQUENCE
Data → Boundary Conditions  → Connections


With this condition it is possible to disconnect some degrees of freedom of points in a structure. Degrees of freedom make reference to local axes of a line.
If the user wants to disconnect some degrees of freedom between two beams, this condition has to be assigned to the union point.
Note, that the conditions are only applicable to beams and not to shells.
