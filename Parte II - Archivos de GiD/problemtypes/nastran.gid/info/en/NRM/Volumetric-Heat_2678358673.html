Nastran reference manual : Volumetric Heat

MENU SEQUENCE:

Data → Loads → Thermal Loads

This condition defines a volumetric heat distribution. The volumetric heat condition plays the same role of gravity in Static Analysis.

 It can be assigned to lines, surfaces and volumes. To apply this condition is mandatory to assign a heat boundary condition to define geometric properties of the elements, i.e. volume of linear elements equal to length x section area.
